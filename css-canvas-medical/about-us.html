Our Story

Andrew founded Canvas in 2015 after years of seeing his wife Anna - a family
nurse practicioner - sacrifice family time in the evenings to "finish" charting. It
never ended.

Andrew knew that the answer wasn't to go out and do it alone. He started to fill the
gaps in his experience. Seven practicing clinicians formed the lcinical advisory
board - rather than leave practice to work full0time with Canvas, they used
and reviewed product changes in their practices. Out Medical firector, Dr.Zimmerman,
joined when he saw the potential of Canvas to help independent practices like his
praactice, Temescal Creek Medicine in Oakland, CA. Julian joined Canvas after
working at Medicare and seeing the failures of current technology to keep up with
increasing regulatory demands without burdening clinicians. Joe and a world class
group of software developers joibed to accelerate our ability to listen to clinician.
feedback and contasntly improve the product.

With Canvas, we created a fundametally different way of charting that comletely
eliminates the need to menus and pop-ups. The name of the company describes
the charting experience - it feels like a blank canvas where you can tell the
patient's story and seamlessly complete the notes, orders, and chart review on a single,
unified screen. With seamlessly, integrated population health, billing, and practice
management, Canvas is a reimagined platform for the moden primary care
practice.
